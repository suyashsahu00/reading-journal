## 📌 About This File
This is a space where I document the most confusing topics I face in my life. These are questions that lead to "Analysis Paralysis"—where the more I think, the deeper the loop becomes. 

I use this to:
- Write down my own confusion to process it.
- Discuss these topics with others.
- Invite others to share their own confusing topics for discussion.

---

## 🧐 Discussion Topics

### 🛤️ The Lone Journey vs. Connection
> *"If you have to travel to death alone, and face the most difficult and challenging phases of your life alone, does it really matter to connect with anyone in life? If not, then tell me why."*
> — Suyash Sahu, March 16, 2026

### 💰 The Paradox of Greed
> *"Why do you think greed is bad? Or why do you think greed is good? Won't having absolutely no greed achieve anything for you or get you anywhere in life?"*
> — Suyash Sahu, March 16, 2026

### 🔗 Bandhan (Bondage) vs. Saath (Companionship)
> *"बंधन में बँधना सब को है... पर शायद सच में, कोई किसी का साथ नहीं देना चाहता। क्या बंधन में बँधना ज़्यादा ज़रूरी है, या साथ देना? उस बंधन का क्या करोगे, जिसमें साथ ही न हो? और अगर साथ हो — हमेशा का — तो क्या बंधन की ज़रूरत है?"*
> — Suyash Sahu, May 03, 2026
