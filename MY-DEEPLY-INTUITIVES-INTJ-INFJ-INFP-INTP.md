## 🧠 The Four Deeply Intuitives

### 🏛️ INTJ — The Architect
> *The rarest of strategists. Visionary, private, and ruthlessly independent.*
> Beneath the cold exterior lives a mind that never stops designing the future.

---

### 🌊 INFJ — The Counselor
> *The rarest of all types. Paradoxically warm yet deeply private.*
> They see through you before you open your mouth — and they still choose to stay.

---

### 🌸 INFP — The Mediator
> *A quiet fire. Idealistic, deeply feeling, and loyal to their inner world.*
> They don't show their real self easily — but when they do, it's the most genuine thing you'll ever witness.

---

### 🔭 INTP — The Logician
> *A living paradox — emotionally distant yet infinitely curious about human depth.*
> They'll debate the universe at 2 AM but struggle to say "I miss you."

**Deeply Intuitives — INTJ, INFJ, INFP, INTP**

To all the INs, please stop seeking surface-level connections with anyone in your life. Stop letting random people into your life.

Remember, you are not here to please most people. The kind of devotion, the kind of charm, the kind of intuition, the kind of understanding you have about another person — just by catching a glimpse of their eyes, a glimpse of their body language — you get to know so much about them. You can tell them why they are doing what they're doing, what's going on in their mind. You can even thoughtfully read their heart.

But intuitives, stop right there.

Ask yourself — is the person standing in front of you truly deserving?
Is the person standing in front of you offering a surface-level connection, or a real, genuine one?

If there is any *if*, *but*, *else* — or even 1% of confusion, of *no*, of *na* — about the person in front of you, then please leave them as soon as possible. No matter what. No matter how.

Remember, INs — your energy is very genuine. You thrive to give all your immense devotion to others, but you hardly receive even half of that in return. Not because they aren't trying, not because they can't — but because the kind of quality you seek, they hardly possess. They will require a lot of life lessons that you have already learned, lived by, and many of those lessons they will carry into the future too, because there is no such need for them at this moment.

Don't waste your time in surface-level connections — not even 1%. Do you know why? As time passes, a sudden cold breeze of wind is going to touch you… a sudden spark of sunlight when you are underneath a tree, its leaves overhead above you. When any natural or cosmic event happens, you're suddenly going to get lost in your mind. You're going to recall every event from the past and present that happened to you — with date, day, time, place, person, background, wind, dawn, and dusk — each and everything. And as you feel all of this, you're going to realize that in your entire life, you have put yourself through so much pain just to prove yourself to others who were never deserving enough. You're going to regret it — *"What did I just do? And why the heck did I do that for them? Why? They never asked me. But why did I still give and give, without any hope of receiving anything?"* — and why, after a long period, when you started deceiving your heart, your brain, and your soul into believing they were genuine like you.

You are striving to find genuineness in 1 — not 2, not 3, not 5, not 10, not so many people — but you have hardly found any.

You start becoming a hypocrite to your own heart — *"See, I have found a genuine friend, a genuine connection."*

But the villa or mansion of your heart that looks so gorgeous, so astonishing, so glamorous — it gets completely washed away. Not because it wasn't that glamorous, not because it wasn't carrying a balanced foundation — but the most essential thing that your mansion is calling for is the other person. The so-called friend, the so-called beloved genuine connection that you are striving for.

That mansion cannot only be built by your 100% effort, deeply intuitive — it is built with both your and the other person's hands, their brain, their heart, and most importantly, their soul. Forget about their soul and heart — you are not even getting their willing hand to build your shared mansion.

You keep trying, telling yourself *"this connection is possible, this is genuine"* — but no, INs, no no no. A genuine connection requires both of your efforts, both of your presence. Stop letting just anyone into your life or craving any kind of connection. Yes, you are mysterious. Yes, you are charming. Yes, when you feel safe with someone, you talk to them so freely, so warmly, so genuinely that they feel they are not even talking to you — but talking to their own heart, which they had stopped listening to for so many years.

And after you put in that effort, that connection, that intuition — the same must come from the other side too. That is genuineness — not sophisticated, not hyper-unrealistic, not what your so-called beloved English term calls *romanticized*.

Remember, INs — the genuine connection you are seeking can only come from another deeply intuitive, another person who is whole within themselves, who is complete within themselves. They are with you not because they *want* something from you — rather, they are with you because they *want to be* with you.

And do you know how you can become genuine for them? By completing the wholeness within yourself.

Striving and connecting with others — not because you or the other person wants anything from each other, or because you both are dependent on each other — but because you are there for each other. You are meant to be lifelong. You are meant to be holy. You are meant to be the cosmic cycle that exists between the two of you.

Be there and be with them — not because you both are striving for something from each other, but because you are *meant to be*.

Your **जिद**, Your stubbornness, your will — God has shaped you so that you don't rely on each other out of need, but you *want* to be with each other. You have realized that having even one genuine connection is far, far better than three, six, or more surface-level ones.

A writer? An observer? A noticer? An intuitive? INTJ? INFJ? INFP? INTP? A deeply intuitive?

A gem — among the kind of gem that you are desiring for. The kind of gem that is desperately praying for you to find genuineness in your life. The genuine connection that your brain, your heart, and most importantly, your soul is waiting for.
